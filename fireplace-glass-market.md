<p><p>The fireplace glass market primarily features two types: ceramic glass and tempered glass. Ceramic glass is designed to withstand high temperatures and offers excellent thermal insulation, making it ideal for high-efficiency fireplaces. It provides a clear view of the flames while enhancing safety. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is heat-treated for strength and safety, shattering into small pieces when broken to minimize injury risks. Both types cater to aesthetic and functional needs in modern fireplace designs.</p></p>
