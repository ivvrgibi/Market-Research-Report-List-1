<p><p>Rooftop solar photovoltaic (PV) installations primarily consist of two market types: crystalline silicon PV and thin-film PV. Crystalline silicon PV, known for its high efficiency and durability, is widely used in residential and commercial rooftops, making up the majority of the market share. In contrast, thin-film PV offers a lightweight and flexible alternative, making it suitable for unconventional surfaces and installations with space constraints. Both types contribute to the growing trend of sustainable energy solutions in urban areas.</p></p>
