<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiology Treatment Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment</li><li>Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Radiology Treatment Equipment market encompasses three primary types: </p><p>1. **External Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** delivers radiation from outside the body, targeting tumors while sparing surrounding tissue, commonly utilizing machines like linear accelerators.</p><p>2. **Internal Beam Radiotherapy Equipment** involves placing radioactive sources directly inside or near the tumor, enhancing localized treatment effectiveness, often through brachytherapy.</p><p>3. **Systemic Radiotherapy Equipment** administers radioactive substances via injection or ingestion, allowing the treatment to target cancer cells throughout the body, capitalizing on their capacity to accumulate in specific tissues.</p></p>
