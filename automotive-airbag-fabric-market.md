<p><strong>The Automotive Airbag Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Airbag Fabric</li><li>OPW (One-Piece Woven) Airbag Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive airbag fabric market features two primary types: Flat Airbag Fabric and OPW (One-Piece Woven) Airbag Fabric. Flat Airbag Fabric typically consists of multiple layers sewn together, offering reliable protection and durability. In contrast, OPW Airbag Fabric is made using a seamless, one-piece weaving technique, providing enhanced strength and reduced weight, which improves overall airbag deployment efficiency. Both types are essential in ensuring passenger safety during collisions, reflecting innovations in automotive safety technology.</p></p>
