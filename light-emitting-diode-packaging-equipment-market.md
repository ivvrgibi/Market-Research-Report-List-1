<p><p>The Light Emitting Diode (LED) packaging equipment market encompasses various processes essential for LED production. Die singulation involves cutting semiconductor wafers into individual chips for packaging. Die attachment secures these chips onto substrates, ensuring optimal thermal and electrical conductivity. LED testing assesses performance and quality before final assembly. Permanent bonding solidifies connections between components, while substrate separation focuses on isolating substrates for better efficiency. Together, these processes enhance LED reliability and performance in applications across diverse industries.</p></p>
