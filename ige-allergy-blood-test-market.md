<p><p>The IgE allergy blood test market includes several key testing methods: ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is widely used for its simplicity and reliability in detecting specific IgE antibodies. Fluorescent Enzyme Immunoassays (FEIA) enhance sensitivity and allow simultaneous testing of multiple allergens. Chemiluminescent Immunoassays utilize light-emitting reactions for improved accuracy and lower detection limits. These methods cater to diverse clinical needs, driving advancements and increasing adoption in allergy diagnosis and management.</p></p>
