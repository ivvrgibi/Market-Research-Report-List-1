<p><p>Foamed plastics insulation products encompass various types, each with unique properties. Polyurethane foam offers excellent thermal insulation and versatility, making it popular in residential and commercial applications. Polystyrene, available in expanded (EPS) and extruded (XPS) forms, provides lightweight insulation with moisture resistance. Polyisocyanurate features high thermal performance and is often used in roofing systems. Phenolic foam offers superior fire resistance and low thermal conductivity but is less common. Other materials may include biodegradable options, catering to eco-conscious markets.</p></p>
