<p><p>The ammonia scrubbers market is categorized into two main types: dry scrubbers and wet scrubbers. Dry scrubbers utilize solid absorbents or chemical reactions to remove ammonia from gases without the need for water, making them suitable for space-limited environments. In contrast, wet scrubbers employ liquids to capture ammonia, allowing for efficient removal and neutralization of gases. Both types serve crucial roles in industrial applications, environmental management, and air quality control, addressing the need to mitigate ammonia emissions effectively.</p></p>
