<p><p>The commercial dry cleaning machine market mainly features two types: single solvent and multi-solvent dry cleaning machines. Single solvent machines use one specific solvent, typically perchloroethylene, making them simpler and often more cost-effective for businesses focusing on conventional cleaning. In contrast, multi-solvent machines can utilize various solvents, providing versatility and enabling businesses to cater to a wider range of fabrics and cleaning needs. This flexibility can enhance cleaning efficacy and meet diverse customer preferences.</p></p>
