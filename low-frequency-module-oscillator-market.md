<p><p>The Low Frequency Module Oscillator market comprises various types, including Voltage-Controlled Oscillators (VCOs), Temperature-Compensated Oscillators (TCXOs), and other specialized oscillators. VCOs allow output frequency to be adjusted by varying input voltage, making them ideal for tuning applications. TCXOs maintain stable frequency despite temperature changes, enhancing performance in precision applications. Other types may include crystal oscillators and digital oscillators, each offering unique features tailored to different industries, such as telecommunications, aerospace, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
