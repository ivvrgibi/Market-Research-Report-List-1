<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Plastic</li><li>Thermosetting Plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastics (GFRP) composite market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting plastics. Thermoplastic GFRPs are characterized by their ability to be reshaped upon heating, offering recyclability and ease of processing. In contrast, thermosetting GFRPs form a rigid structure that hardens permanently after curing, providing superior thermal and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction, capitalizing on their lightweight, strong, and durable properties.</p></p>
