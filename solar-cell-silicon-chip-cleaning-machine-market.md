<p><p>The solar cell silicon chip cleaning machine market is segmented into three main types: ultrasonic cleaning, high-pressure cleaning, and others. Ultrasonic cleaning utilizes high-frequency sound waves to generate cavitation bubbles, effectively removing contaminants from silicon surfaces. High-pressure cleaning employs powerful jets of water to dislodge dirt and debris, ensuring a thorough cleanse. Other cleaning methods may include chemical treatments or manual processes. Each type offers varying efficiency and effectiveness in maintaining the performance and lifespan of solar cells.</p></p>
