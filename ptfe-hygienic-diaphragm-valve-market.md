<p><p>The PTFE Hygienic Diaphragm Valve market is primarily categorized into Weir Type and Weirless Radial Diaphragm Type valves. Weir Type valves feature a raised structure that directs fluid flow, providing effective sealing and control, making them suitable for various hygienic applications. In contrast, Weirless Radial Diaphragm Type valves employ a flat diaphragm design that minimizes dead zones, enhancing cleanability and efficiency. Both types are essential in industries such as food, pharmaceuticals, and biotechnology, where hygiene and precision are critical.</p></p>
