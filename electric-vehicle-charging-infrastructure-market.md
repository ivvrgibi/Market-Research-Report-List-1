<p><strong>The Electric Vehicle Charging Infrastructure Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Alternating Current (AC) Charger</li><li>Direct Current (DC) Charger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric vehicle charging infrastructure market comprises two primary types of chargers: Alternating Current (AC) chargers and Direct Current (DC) chargers. AC chargers are typically used for home and public charging stations, supplying electricity that the vehicle’s onboard converter transforms to recharge the battery. They are slower but more cost-effective. In contrast, DC chargers provide high-speed charging by delivering direct current to the battery, allowing for rapid recharging at commercial locations, making them ideal for long-distance travel and fleet operations.</p></p>
