<p><p>The prefilled IV flush syringe market primarily consists of saline IV flush syringes and heparin IV flush syringes. Saline IV flush syringes are used to maintain catheter patency and prevent clotting, ensuring proper drug delivery. Heparin IV flush syringes contain the anticoagulant heparin, designed to prevent thrombus formation in indwelling catheters. Both types enhance efficiency and safety in healthcare settings, reducing the risk of medication errors and streamlining the flushing process in various medical procedures.</p></p>
