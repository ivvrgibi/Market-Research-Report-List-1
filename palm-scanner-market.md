<p><strong>The Palm Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Scanning</li><li>Vein Scanning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The palm scanner market encompasses two primary types: optical scanning and vein scanning. Optical scanning relies on capturing high-resolution images of the palm's surface, utilizing patterns, lines, and features for identification. In contrast, vein scanning leverages the unique patterns of veins beneath the skin, offering enhanced security and accuracy through infrared light technology. Both types are increasingly adopted in various sectors, including healthcare, security, and access control, driven by the need for reliable biometric authentication methods.</p></p>
