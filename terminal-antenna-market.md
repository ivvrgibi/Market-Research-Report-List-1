<p><p>The terminal antenna market primarily comprises two main types: base station antennas and terminal antennas. Base station antennas are designed for high-capacity communication, facilitating signals between the base station and multiple user devices, often used in cellular networks. In contrast, terminal antennas are typically employed in individual user devices, such as smartphones and laptops, providing direct connectivity to communication networks. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing signal quality and network efficiency across various applications.</p></p>
