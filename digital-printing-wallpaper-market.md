<p><strong>The Digital Printing Wallpaper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Electrophotography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital printing wallpaper market primarily features inkjet and electrophotography technologies. Inkjet printing uses tiny droplets of ink to create detailed images on wallpaper, allowing for vibrant colors and custom designs. In contrast, electrophotography, commonly known as laser printing, utilizes toner and a photoconductor to produce sharp, high-resolution prints. Both methods cater to diverse design preferences and applications, offering versatility in texture and finish, making them suitable for residential and commercial environments in the wallpaper industry.</p></p>
