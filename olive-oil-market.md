<p><p>The olive oil market comprises three main types: Extra Virgin Olive Oil, which is the highest quality, cold-pressed and free of defects, offering rich flavor and health benefits. Regular Olive Oil is a refined product blended with a small amount of extra virgin, making it more stable for cooking but with milder taste and lower nutritional value. Olive Pomace Oil is extracted from the leftover olive pulp after the first extraction and is often used in industrial food production due to its lower cost.</p></p>
