Fully automatic models operate independently, streamlining the sectioning process and enhancing throughput, ideal for high-volume labs. Manual microtomes require operator skill for cutting, providing flexibility and precision but demanding more time and effort. Each type serves distinct user needs in research, pathology, and diagnostics, influencing market dynamics and preferences.</p></p>
