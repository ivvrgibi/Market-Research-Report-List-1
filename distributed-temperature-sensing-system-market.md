<p><ul><li>Single-Mode Fiber</li><li>Multimode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS) system market is categorized into Single-Mode Fiber (SMF) and Multimode Fiber (MMF) types. SMF offers higher sensitivity and longer sensing distance, making it suitable for applications in oil and gas, power generation, and environmental monitoring. In contrast, MMF is ideal for shorter-range applications due to its larger core, allowing for multiple light paths, which results in greater temperature measurement in confined spaces. Each type serves specific industry needs based on performance and distance requirements.</p></p>
