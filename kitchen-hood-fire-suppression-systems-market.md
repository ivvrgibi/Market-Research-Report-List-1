<p><p>Kitchen hood fire suppression systems are crucial for mitigating fire risks in commercial kitchens. The market is primarily divided into two types: manual trigger and thermal trigger systems. Manual trigger systems require human intervention to activate the suppression mechanism, offering control during emergencies. In contrast, thermal trigger systems automatically engage when a certain temperature threshold is exceeded, providing rapid response to fires without requiring immediate human action. Both systems aim to enhance kitchen safety by effectively extinguishing fires at their source.</p></p>
