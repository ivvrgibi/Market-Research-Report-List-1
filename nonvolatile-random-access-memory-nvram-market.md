<p><p>Nonvolatile Random Access Memory (NVRAM) retains data even without power, making it essential for various applications. The market comprises several types, including SRAM (Static RAM), which offers fast access speeds but is costlier, making it suitable for cache memory. MRAM (Magnetoresistive RAM) combines speed and nonvolatility, leveraging magnetic states for data storage, ideal for high-performance applications. FRAM (Ferroelectric RAM) utilizes ferroelectric materials, providing low power consumption and high endurance, making it effective for smart cards and industrial applications.</p></p>
