Back pressure type micrometers gauge the pressure differential caused by air flow restrictions, ensuring accurate measurements in pneumatic systems. Other variations may include advanced digital models or those designed for specific industrial applications, catering to diverse measurement needs in manufacturing and engineering sectors.</p></p>
