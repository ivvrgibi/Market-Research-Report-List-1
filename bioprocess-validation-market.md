<p><p>The bioprocess validation market comprises several key types, including Extractables and Leachables (E&L) testing, Integrity Testing, and Microbiology Testing. E&L testing assesses the safety of materials that may release harmful substances into biologics. Integrity Testing ensures the sterility and functionality of filter systems and containers, which is critical for product safety. Microbiology Testing evaluates the presence of microorganisms in biopharmaceutical processes, ensuring compliance with safety standards and maintaining product efficacy throughout manufacturing.</p></p>
