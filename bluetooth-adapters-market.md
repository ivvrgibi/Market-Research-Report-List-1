<p><p>The Bluetooth adapters market is primarily divided into single-band and dual-band adapters. Single-band Bluetooth adapters operate exclusively on the 2.4 GHz frequency, offering basic connectivity for devices like keyboards and mice, but may experience interference in crowded environments. In contrast, dual-band Bluetooth adapters support both 2.4 GHz and 5 GHz frequencies, providing enhanced performance, reduced interference, and improved range. This versatility makes dual-band adapters preferable for advanced applications such as gaming and streaming, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
