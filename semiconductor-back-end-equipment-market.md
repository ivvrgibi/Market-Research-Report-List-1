<p><p>The semiconductor back-end equipment market encompasses various essential types used in the final stages of chip production. Wafer inspection equipment ensures quality by detecting defects. Dicing equipment cuts wafers into individual chips. Bonding equipment connects the chips to their packages, while molding equipment protects them with a resin coating. Encapsulation equipment provides additional protection and structural support. Other equipment may include testing and packaging tools, all crucial for ensuring reliable and efficient semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
