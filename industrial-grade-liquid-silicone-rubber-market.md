<p><p>The Industrial Grade Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) market is segmented into three viscosity types: low, medium, and high viscosity. Low viscosity LSR is ideal for intricate mold designs and offers excellent flow characteristics, making it suitable for detailed applications. Medium viscosity LSR balances flow and strength, commonly used for general-purpose applications in automotive and consumer goods. High viscosity LSR, characterized by its thicker consistency, provides enhanced durability and is often employed for heavy-duty applications, including seals and gaskets.</p></p>
