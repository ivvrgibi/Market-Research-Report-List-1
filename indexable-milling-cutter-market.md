<p><p>The indexable milling cutter market is classified into several types based on the material used. Carbide cutters are known for their durability and hardness, making them suitable for various materials. PCD (polycrystalline diamond) and CBN (cubic boron nitride) cutters are ideal for machining hard and abrasive materials, offering exceptional wear resistance. Ceramics provide high-speed cutting capabilities and are used for specialized applications due to their high-temperature resistance. Other materials may include high-speed steel or hybrid options, catering to diverse machining needs.</p></p>
