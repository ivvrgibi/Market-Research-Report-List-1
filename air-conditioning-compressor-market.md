<p><ul><li>Piston Compressor</li><li>Rotary Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning compressor market primarily features two main types: piston compressors and rotary compressors. Piston compressors utilize a reciprocating mechanism to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and robust cooling capability, making them suitable for larger systems. In contrast, rotary compressors rely on rotating elements to compress refrigerant, providing quieter operation and compact design, ideal for residential and small commercial applications. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences, influencing their market demand and usage within the HVAC industry.</p></p>
