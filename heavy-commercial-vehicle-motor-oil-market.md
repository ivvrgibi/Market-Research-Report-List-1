<p><p>The Heavy Commercial Vehicle Motor Oil market primarily consists of two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from refining crude oil and offers good lubrication and protection for heavy-duty engines, but may break down more quickly under extreme conditions. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is artificially produced and provides superior performance, improved fuel efficiency, and enhanced engine protection under high temperatures, making it increasingly popular among heavy commercial vehicle operators looking for durability and efficiency.</p></p>
