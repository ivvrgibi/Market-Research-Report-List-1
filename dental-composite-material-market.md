<p><ul><li>Macrofill Composites</li><li>Microfill Composites</li><li>Hybrid Composites</li><li>Nanofill Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental composite material market comprises various types based on particle size and performance characteristics. Macrofill composites contain larger particles, offering strength and durability but less polishability. Microfill composites feature smaller particles for superior aesthetic finish and polish but can be less durable. Hybrid composites combine both particle sizes, providing a balance of strength and aesthetics. Nanofill composites utilize nanoparticles for exceptional polishability and minimal wear, making them ideal for anterior restorations. These types cater to diverse clinical needs in dentistry.</p></p>
