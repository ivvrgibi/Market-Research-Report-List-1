<p><p>The submarine communication cables market is primarily divided into two types: unarmoured and armoured cables. Unarmoured submarine communication cables are lightweight and flexible, designed for shallow waters and regions with less environmental threat. In contrast, armoured submarine cables feature protective layers, making them resilient against physical damage from marine activities and harsh underwater conditions, ideal for deeper oceans and areas with heavy fishing or shipping traffic.
