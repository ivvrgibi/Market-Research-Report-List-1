<p><p>Metal pallet pooling is a service model that involves the rental or sharing of metal pallets among multiple users, rather than purchasing these assets outright. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as the integration of tracking systems and IoT, are enhancing the management of pallet pools, allowing for better inventory control and reduced losses. <p><p>The metal pallet pooling market involves two primary types: pallet rental and pallet pooling. Pallet rental refers to short-term leasing of metal pallets for specific needs, allowing companies to access pallets without ownership costs. In contrast, pallet pooling involves sharing a pool of metal pallets among multiple users, promoting cost efficiency and sustainability. This system reduces the need for each company to invest in large inventories, while ensuring a consistent supply of pallets for their logistical operations.</p></p>
