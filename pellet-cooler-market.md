<p><strong>The Pellet Cooler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Counterflow Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pellet cooler market offers three primary types: horizontal, vertical, and counterflow coolers. Horizontal coolers feature a cylindrical design, promoting efficient heat exchange and ease of maintenance. Vertical coolers utilize gravity to facilitate the downward movement of pellets, enhancing cooling efficiency while minimizing footprint. Counterflow coolers operate by directing cooled air upward against the flow of pellets, ensuring optimal temperature reduction. Each type serves various industries, optimizing pellet quality and enhancing processing efficiency based on specific operational needs.</p></p>
