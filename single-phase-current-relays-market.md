<p><p>The Single-Phase Current Relays market includes Under-Current and Over-Current Detection types, essential for electrical safety and equipment protection. Under-Current Detection relays monitor current levels, triggering alarms or actions when the current drops below a preset threshold, preventing potential equipment damage. Conversely, Over-Current Detection relays identify excessive current flow, initiating protective measures such as circuit disconnection to avoid overheating and failures. Both types enhance system reliability and efficiency, catering to various industries like manufacturing and renewable energy.</p></p>
