<p><p>The offshore floating rig market includes three primary types: jackup rigs, semi-submersibles, and drillships. Jackup rigs are shallow-water platforms that can be raised above sea level on legs. Semi-submersibles are stable floating structures anchored to the seabed, suitable for deeper waters. Drillships are specialized vessels equipped with drilling equipment, ideal for ultra-deepwater operations. Each type is designed for specific environmental conditions and drilling depths, catering to the diverse needs of the oil and gas industry in offshore exploration and production.</p></p>
