<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chromium target markets primarily consist of two types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, uniform surfaces used for applications requiring consistent deposition, such as in semiconductor manufacturing and optics. They deliver even coating over large areas. In contrast, rotating targets are cylindrical and designed to continuously rotate during the deposition process, ensuring even wear and longer lifespan. This type is often utilized in high-throughput applications, enhancing efficiency and uniformity in thin-film production.</p></p>
