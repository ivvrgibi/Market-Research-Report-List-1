<p><strong>The Automatic Blood Glucose Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>GDH-FAD</li><li>GOD</li><li>Electrochemistry</li><li>GDH</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Blood Glucose Meter market comprises several types based on the technology used for measuring glucose levels. GDH-FAD (Glucose Dehydrogenase-Flavin Adenine Dinucleotide) is known for its high accuracy and minimal interference from other substances. GOD (Glucose Oxidase) measures glucose levels through an enzymatic reaction, while electrochemistry refers to various electrochemical methods used for detection. GDH (Glucose Dehydrogenase) also employs enzymatic reactions but with different co-factors. "Other" includes alternative technologies and emerging methodologies in glucose monitoring.</p></p>
