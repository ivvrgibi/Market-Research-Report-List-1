<p><strong>The Handheld Pyranometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermopile</li><li>Photovoltaic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld pyranometer market is primarily segmented into two types: thermopile and photovoltaic. Thermopile pyranometers utilize a series of thermocouples to measure solar radiation by detecting temperature differences, offering high precision in various environmental conditions. In contrast, photovoltaic pyranometers convert solar energy directly into electrical signals, providing a quicker response time and ease of use for portable applications. Both types serve essential roles in solar energy assessments, agricultural monitoring, and meteorological studies, catering to different user preferences and application needs.</p></p>
