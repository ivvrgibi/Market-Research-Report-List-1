<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Solid-state</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile LiDAR Scanner market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical and solid-state systems. Mechanical LiDAR scanners utilize rotating components to capture 3D data, providing high-resolution imagery over large areas but can be heavier and more complex. In contrast, solid-state LiDAR scanners employ fewer moving parts, resulting in a more compact design, increased reliability, and lower maintenance needs. Solid-state systems are often favored for their durability and ease of integration into various mobile platforms.</p></p>
