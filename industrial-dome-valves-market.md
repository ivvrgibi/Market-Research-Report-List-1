<p><p>The industrial dome valves market comprises various types based on actuation methods: manual, electric, pneumatic, and hydraulic. Manual valves rely on human operation for control, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Electric valves use electric actuators, providing precise control and automation for complex processes. Pneumatic valves utilize compressed air, ensuring rapid operation suitable for high-speed applications. Hydraulic valves harness fluid pressure for powerful actuation, ideal for heavy-duty industrial tasks. Each type caters to specific operational needs and process requirements in diverse industries.</p></p>
