<p><ul><li>Feedforward Active Noise Canceling</li><li>Feedback ANC</li><li>Hybrid ANC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) earphone market features three main types: Feedforward ANC, Feedback ANC, and Hybrid ANC. Feedforward ANC utilizes external microphones to detect ambient noise and generate counteracting sound waves, offering effective noise reduction in quieter environments. Feedback ANC, on the other hand, uses internal microphones to monitor sound within the earbud and adjust accordingly, providing better performance in noisier settings. Hybrid ANC combines both methods for optimal noise cancellation, delivering superior performance across varying sound environments.</p></p>
