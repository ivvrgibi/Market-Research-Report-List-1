<p><p>The Blood Filter Market can be categorized into three primary types based on the type of transfusion. Whole Blood Transfusion involves filtering unprocessed blood for surgery or trauma, ensuring the removal of contaminants. Platelet Transfusion focuses on filtering platelets to treat conditions like thrombocytopenia, enhancing clotting ability. Red Cell Transfusion targets the separation of red blood cells for patients with anemia or significant blood loss, ensuring safe and effective transfusion while minimizing transfusion reactions and infections.</p></p>
